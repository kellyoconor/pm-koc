# Sprint Retro and Lessons Capture

## Schedule
End of every sprint

## What It Does
Facilitates sprint retrospective, captures lessons learned, and feeds insights back into the system so future sprints improve.

## Workflow
1. Run retro skill — what went well, what didn't, action items
2. Capture lessons in iterate-lessons-log
3. Review previous retro action items — were they completed?
4. Update refinement notes for next sprint
5. Feed insights into risk-assessor to update risk patterns

## Skills Used
- execution/retro
- execution/iterate-lessons-log
- execution/iterate-refinement-notes
- execution/pre-mortem (update risk patterns)

## Output
Sprint Retro Summary:
- What went well (keep doing)
- What didn't (stop or change)
- Action items with owners and due dates
- Previous action items: completed / still open
- Patterns emerging across retros
- Updated risk watch list

## Trigger
`/retro` or automated at sprint end
