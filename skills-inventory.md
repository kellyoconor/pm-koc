**Key Takeaways:**

1. **lenny-skills has the broadest coverage** (84 skills) with heavy emphasis on soft skills, leadership, career, and sales domains that no other repo covers.
2. **deanpeters has the deepest PM-craft skills** — unique finance/SaaS metrics suite, leadership transitions (3 levels), validation probes, and interactive workshops.
3. **product-on-purpose is the most structured** — organized by product lifecycle phase (Discover/Define/Develop/Deliver/Measure/Iterate) with the strongest engineering-adjacent skills (ADRs, spikes, instrumentation specs).
4. **pm_koc has the strongest discovery pipeline** — unique assumption identification and prioritization at two product stages (new/existing), plus brainstorming for both ideas and experiments.
5. **productskills is the most opinionated** — small but each skill names specific frameworks (Shape Up, Teresa Torres, April Dunford).
6. **claude-skills has unmatched breadth outside PM** — 200+ skills across engineering, C-suite advisory, marketing, compliance, but only 8 PM-specific.
7. **pm-claude-code-setup is a starter kit** — all 6 skills are subsets of what other repos provide more deeply.

**Most-covered PM activities (6+ repos):** PRD writing, competitive analysis, user research/interviews.
**Least-covered PM areas:** Accessibility, data infrastructure, compliance, AI governance, revenue forecasting, remote team management.
